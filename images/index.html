IEDC | STM

IEDC Cell Of St Thomas College of Engineering & Technology, Suvapuram, Kannur Was launched on 3rd November 2016 by Our Principal Dr. Shinu Mathew John(Principal). Objectives of IEDC

  • To act as an institutional mechanism for providing various services including information on all aspects of enterprise building to budding S&T entrepreneurs.
  • To create Entrepreneurial culture in the Parent Institution and other institutions in the region.
  • To inculcate a culture of innovation driven entrepreneurship through student projects.

    History

    2017 (April) -

    Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) was inaugurated by Mr. C Rajan (Chairman, Kerala Dinesh Cooperative Society) by making a call which initiated a contraption mechanism, made by our students, which eventually led to Logo and video launch.

    2017 (May) -

    Students from S4 Mechanical (Jishnu K, Amrith Raj, Aadarsh TV, Sourav V and Yadhukrisnan CA) has been shortlisted for the final stage of Project Competition conducted by KTU. Their project is one among the 6 projects selected for the finals.

    2018 (Sep) -

    IEDC in association with ICT Academy and KSUM organized an inter college level 2 days workshop for aspiring students startup on “Foundation skills on Entrepreneurship” on 26th September to 27th September 2018. 32 students from different colleges including STM participated in the workshop

    Services Of IEDC | STM

    • Community Formation

    Forming a community who can pitch their innovative ideas. Community of Infinte Innovatice ideas.

    • Research

    Researching about the Ideas presented by the Community and focusing on their drawbacks and rectifying it.

    • Approval Of the Innovation

    After Rectifying the Drawbacks the idea presented will be approved by the Head Of Members.

    • Competition

    The Innovation will be competiting in Summits conducted by Universities or Kerala Startup Mission Cell.

    • Presentation

    On Competitions the approved ideas will be presented by the Presentation Team of respected Projects.

    • Funding

    Best projects in the Summits Will Be funded by Kerala Start up mission to reach the innovation to the Society.
